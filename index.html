How the pool works
Make your picks before the tournament kicks off on June 11. Points are awarded for every correct prediction — the later the round, the more they're worth.

You'll pick 3 teams to advance from each of the 12 groups, then eliminate 4 of your 12 third-place teams to get to 32. From there, pick your way through the knockout rounds all the way to a winner.

Points double with each round, so there's always something to play for right up to the Final.
