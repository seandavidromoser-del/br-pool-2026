How the pool works

Make your picks before the tournament kicks off on June 11. Points are awarded for every correctly predicted team that advances. The later the round, the more they're worth.

You'll draft which 32 teams you think advance out of the group stage: 2 from each group, plus 8 wildcards. Then you whittle that down to 16, 8, 4, 2, and finally a champion.

Points double each round, so even a rough group stage can be clawed back. That's the beauty of it.
