How the pool works
Make your picks before the tournament kicks off on June 11. Points are awarded for every correct prediction — the later the round, the more they're worth.

Pick 3 teams from each group, eliminate 4 third-place teams to get to 32, then fill out the bracket round by round. The bracket follows the official FIFA structure, so teams from the same group can never meet before the Round of 16 at the earliest.

Points double each round, so there's always something to play for right up to the Final.
